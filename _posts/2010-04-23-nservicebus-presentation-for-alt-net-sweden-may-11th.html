I'll be giving an introduction to NServiceBus for the Alt.net usergroup in Stockholm on the evening of May 11:th. The presentation will focus on how NServiceBus can help you build more robust and scalable applications by composing them of autonomous components that interact using asynchronous messaging patterns. We'll finish with a coding session where we build a small NServiceBus solution together.

Details and signup can be found at http://altnetnservicebus.doattend.com/

Kudos to Emil Cardell and Valtech for sponsoring the event with food and beverages!
