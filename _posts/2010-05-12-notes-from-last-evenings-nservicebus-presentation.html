I spent last evening giving a presentation/workshop on NServiceBus for the alt.net guys in Stockholm. I had a great time and was really impressed with the skill level of the audience. There was a lot of great questions and discussions that made the evening even more valuable for me. Hopefully everyone left with a better understanding of what NServiceBus is and what problems it's meant to solve.
