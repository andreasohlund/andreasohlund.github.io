>I while ago I posted my thoughts about cross service transactions (or rather my disbelief in them). I just read Juval Lowy's article on WCF transaction propagation in the May issue of MSDN magazine and can't se the need for it. I still firmly believe that transactions between services should be avoided at all costs. Arnon Rotem-Gal-Oz's has published some comments that supports my theory, an there fore it must be true :)

Well, WCF is great but I would rather see that they spend some more time on publish/subscribe support instead of building transactional features.
